# Business Understanding

## Project Overview

The Indian quick-commerce industry has grown rapidly, with platforms such as **Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart, BigBasket, and JioMart** competing to provide faster deliveries, better product availability, and improved customer experiences.

This project focuses on **selected Bangalore pincodes** to compare the operational performance of these platforms and identify opportunities to improve delivery efficiency, inventory management, and customer satisfaction through data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problems

| `Business Problem` | `Description` |
|------------------|-------------|
| Delivery Delays | Orders are not always delivered within the promised delivery time (SLA). |
| Product Availability | Frequently ordered products become unavailable because of inventory shortages. |
| Customer Retention | Customers switch to competitors due to poor service quality or inconsistent experiences. |
| Inventory Planning | Dark stores may not maintain inventory according to local customer demand. |
| Business Visibility | Businesses need data-driven insights to improve operational performance across different localities. |

---

## Project Objectives

- Compare the operational performance of India's leading quick-commerce platforms.
- Identify operational factors affecting delivery performance.
- Analyze inventory availability and product demand across selected Bangalore pincodes.
- Understand customer satisfaction using ratings and feedback.
- Generate business insights that support better operational decision-making.

---

## Assumptions

- Bangalore is selected because it is one of India's largest quick-commerce markets.
- The analysis focuses on selected pincodes to keep the project manageable while representing different customer segments.
- Public datasets are used to simulate real-world business scenarios.

---

## Project Limitations

- Real operational databases of Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart, BigBasket, and JioMart are not publicly available.
- Some business information such as weather, traffic, warehouse locations, and live inventory is unavailable in public datasets.
- The project focuses on historical data and descriptive & diagnostic analytics. Predictive analytics is considered future work.
